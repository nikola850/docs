## How RufLab works

RufLab acts as your virtual receptionist. When a customer calls your number, RufLab picks up immediately, understands their request, and takes the right action — whether that's booking an appointment, answering a common question, forwarding an urgent call, or logging a message for later follow-up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure your call logic">
    Use the dashboard to define how your assistant greets callers, what questions it answers, when it books appointments, and which calls get forwarded to you directly — no coding required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your voice and tone">
    Select from professional German voices and customize the greeting, tone, and conversation style to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and go live">
    Make a test call to verify everything works, adjust any details, and activate your assistant. From that point on, every incoming call is handled professionally.
  </Step>
</Steps>
